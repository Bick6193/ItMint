Business Experts Building Business Solutions

Fusing together your best practices and industry expertise with our teams understanding of sound technologies and emerging trends will give you a competitive advantage. Whether we are custom building a stand-alone application for your customers, or a complex and fully integrated path-to-market engine for your sales team, our approach to your project will be low-risk and built using industry best practices and years of experience.

  01 BUSINESS ANALYSIS

Our Business Analysts work with your key personnel and users to interpret core business needs, scope out the supporting system, translate technical issues, and define project details and requirements to ensure that code development begins on the best possible footing. 

 
  02 UI/UX DESIGN

The Itmint UI/UX design team works closely with you and our business analysts to create a stunning visual prototype of the future product. Effective UI/UX design sets the stage for a successful and straightforward development process.

 
  03 DEVELOPMENT

Normally the most resource intensive, longest, and expensive phase, development is arranged whenever possible to provide exciting sneak peaks of working functionality and solid progress through regular weekly customer check-ins. 

 
  04 TESTING

Our Quality Assurance team goes well beyond stomping on bugs to validate all code development against the full customer and BA defined requirements.

 
  05 SUPPORT

Major projects often do not end with a satisfied customer going live with their shiny new code. Long-term support and frequent enhancements from your demanding users can be planned for and provided through affordable support contracts.
